PREFACE

    This handbook, published as Advisory Circular 61-13B, was prepared as a technical manual for applicants who are preparing for their private, commercial, or flight instructor pilot certificates with a helicopter rating. Certificated helicopter flight instructors may find this handbook a valuable aid in training students, since detailed coverage of helicopter aerodynamics, performance, and flight maneuvers are included. Items such as weather, navigation, radio navigation and communications, use of flight information publications, and pertinent Federal Aviation Regulations are not included. These topics, with the exception of regulations, are included in the FAA publication, Pilot's Handbook of Aeronautical Knowledge, AC 61-23 and in other FAA publications listed in the references on page 105.
